How do I get services?

Start by contacting us by email, text, or phone. You can find our contact information in the "Contact Us" section above. Be sure to include your contact information and a discription of the services you will need. We will then contact you to set up a time and date for the assessment.

How long will it take to hear back after the assessment?

In most cases you will hear back from us within a few business days, depending on the complexity of the assessment. We will contact you with an estimate for the services needed and with your approval we will schedule a date/time to complete the services needed.

What should I expect on the day of services?

We will work with you to set up a date and time that will create as little down time as possible for yourself or your business. On the scheduled date/time we will need someone there to provide access to the location. Once we have finished we will provide you with any documentation you might need related to the services completed.
